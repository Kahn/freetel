FAQ

  1. How do I set up my Phone system?
    1. Hover your mouse to get instructions (try the menu to the left).
    2. Analog Ports are detected automatically.
    3. Set up your Network.
    4. Add your IP Phones.
    5. Configure your Voip Line.
    6. The Phone System page gives you a summary of your phone system and tells you how to make calls (hover your mouse).
  2. Where can I find out more about Mini Asterisk: The Mini Asterisk Home Page.
  3. Where can I get Support: You can try the Free Telephony Project Mailing List.
  4. Answer a call on another ringing phone: If you hear another phone ringing and want to answer the call on your phone, just pick up your phone and dial *8. Note an IP phone can pick up a call on a ringing IP phone but not an Analog phone. An Analog pohone can pick up another ringing Analog phone but not an IP phone.
  5. Put a call on hold/transfer: On an IP Phone try the recall or transfer button. On an Analog Phone hold down the hook button for 1 second then dial the new number.
  6. Why another Asterisk GUI I had some specific needs for a light weight, very easy to use GUI for the IP0X line of products. So I scratched the itch. More information here.
